The Well

The Sequence of Events

A broke out during the orientalizing workshop phase of this site. This caused a great deal of damage to the decoration and tiling. The fragments were then incorporated into a fill leveling the ground to the west of the building, and on this leveled ground was built. This well is then dug into the leveled fill. At a later date this well is intentionally filled with large amounts decoration from the archaic phase and sealed.

What Does This Mean?

The presence of fragments from the archaic phase of the site in the fill outside the well does not have to mean that this fill was created after the destruction of the archaic building. During the use of this building had to be made, so the broken fragments were mixed in with the fill. Then, close to the end of the life of the building, the fill is re-excavated and this well is constructed. This line of thinking goes on to help construct a more acurate timeline, and allows placement of a well on this reconstruction.
